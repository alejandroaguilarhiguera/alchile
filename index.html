Jennifer T

Due to COVID I did not have the opportunity to visit Mexico this summer. I was definitely missing the authentic food from Mexico but I could not have been happier after visiting Al Chile Taqueria. Their Horchata is AMAZING! It is even better than a lot of the horchatas I've tried in Mexico. I went in and got some Fajita Tacos as well as some Tacos con queso. I added some of the red sauce and boy it was spicy, so I recommend you accompany with a Large horchata (or drink of your choice but that's my favorite). The tacos where really good. I was also glad to see that the tortilla was nice and soft and the vegetables were fresh. I'm so in love with the horchata that I've even come in just to buy that . The food was great, the service was great, and most importantly the Horchata was incredible!
